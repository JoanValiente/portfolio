About me

I was born in 1998, and since then I have been surrounded by games.  I have been playing since I can remember, from an Atari and a Super Nintendo to today's consoles and PC, going through all the games and consoles that I could afford. I grew up in a culture in which the videogame industry already formed a large part of the entertainment industry. However, there was always something different with me. I was always attracted to labors that allowed me to be creative; like drawing, crafts, music... And it was not different with games. Not only did I play and have a good time, but I liked to look at how the levels were made, what was the most fun for me, how the game could work. That curiosity and passion made me decide, since I was very young, that I wanted to dedicate myself to this industry and provoke in others the same thing that developers of the games I played as a child made me feel.
So, in 2016, I started the bachelor's degree in Game Design and Development at CITM(UPC), and the experience since then has been amazing. I am currently in the last year and I have worked on a lot of different and fun projects. I discovered new passions such as programming, I have developed new skills in areas that I already loved such as design and art, I have met awesome people who share the same love for games and, above all, I have learned a lot and gathered new insights.
